Discover Eden Island, a picturesque paradise nestled in the Red Sea, where crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and serene beaches await. This full-day excursion combines relaxation, exploration, and indulgence for a perfect escape from the ordinary.

Convenient Transfers: Begin your day with 7:30 AM pickup from Hurghada hotels. Transfers from hotels outside Hurghada are also available.

Scenic Sailing: Set sail at 9:15 AM, gliding across the pristine waters aboard a comfortable vessel.

Eden Island Arrival: Arrive at this tranquil haven by 10:15 AM and bask in the beauty of its sandy beaches.

Delicious Snacks: Recharge with light refreshments at 10:30 AM to fuel your energy for the adventure.

Thrilling Snorkeling: Dive into a 45-minute snorkeling session at 12:00 PM to explore vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life.

Sumptuous Lunch: Enjoy an open buffet lunch at 1:00 PM, featuring a variety of mouthwatering dishes.

Leisure Time: Relax on the island, swim, or soak up the sun until 3:00 PM.

Return Journey: Sail back to the marina at 3:45 PM, ending your adventure with serene views.

Included:

  • ✔️ Round-trip transfers from Hurghada hotels.
  • ✔️ Boat trip to Eden Island.
  • ✔️ Snacks and an open buffet lunch.
  • ✔️ Snorkeling equipment (mask, fins, snorkel).
  • ✔️ Assistance from professional guides.

Excluded:

  • ❌ Transfer fees for hotels outside Hurghada (available upon request).
  • ❌ Personal expenses and gratuities.
  • Pickup: 7:30 AM.
  • Return to hotel: Around 5:00 PM.

Do I need snorkeling experience to join this trip?

No prior experience is needed! Our professional guides will assist you, making it suitable for everyone.

Are there shaded areas on Eden Island?

Yes, shaded spots are available to ensure your comfort throughout the day.

What should I bring?

Bring swimwear, a towel, sunscreen, a hat, and a camera to capture the stunning scenery.

Is lunch included in the price?

Yes, a delicious open buffet lunch is included to keep you energized for the day.
